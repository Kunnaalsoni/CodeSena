Who are we?

Apart from being a company ;)

ChildChain is a blockchain solution that solves the problem of fake birth record entries in India. In a country like India, there are a lot of schemes launched for the new born babies, so that the economical backward peopke can benefit from it.

But, there are a lot of people who faking themselves and taking these benefits, exhausting the Government's resources. ChildChain is a solution that focuses on integrating blockchain to make this process more secure and reliable, ensuring that government's resources are properly utilised..
